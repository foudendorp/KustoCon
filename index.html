About the KustoCon

This virtual conference brings together KQL professionals from around the world. Join us for a day of insightful talks, networking, and collaboration.

Agenda

  • 14:00 CEST - Welcome & What is KQL and Why you should learn it by Gianni & Alex
  • 15:00 CEST - Anchored in Innovation - The Story of Kusto by Henning Rauch
  • 16:00 CEST - Threat Hunting with Kusto by Mattias Borg & Stefan Schorling
  • 17:00 CEST - Break
  • 17:30 CEST - Find lateral movement paths using KQL Graph semantics by Fabian Bader
  • 18:30 CEST - The Kusto Approach to Unified Audit Log by Bert-Jan Pals
  • 19:30 CEST - Detection Engineering rabbit holes, Parsing ASN.1 packets with KQL by Olaf Hartong
  • 20:20 CEST - Goodbye
